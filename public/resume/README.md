# Resume

## How to Add Your Resume

1. **Save your resume PDF** to this folder: `public/resume/`
2. **Name it exactly**: `resume.pdf`
3. **Update the link** in `content/data.ts`:
   - Change `contact.resume` from `"#"` to `"/resume/resume.pdf"`

## Steps:

1. Open Finder
2. Navigate to: `/Users/jun/Documents/Info 490 Portfolio/arjunsupra_portfolio/public/resume/`
3. Copy your resume PDF into this folder
4. Rename it to `resume.pdf`
5. Update `content/data.ts` - change `resume: "#"` to `resume: "/resume/resume.pdf"`

The resume will be downloadable when users click the "Resume" link in the top-left corner.
