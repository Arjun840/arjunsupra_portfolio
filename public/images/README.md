# Profile Image

## How to Add Your Photo

1. **Save your photo** to this folder: `public/images/`
2. **Name it exactly**: `profile.jpg`
   - If your photo is a PNG, rename it to `profile.jpg` or update the code to use `.png`
3. **Recommended size**: 400x400px or larger (square or portrait works best)
4. **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP

## Steps:

1. Open Finder
2. Navigate to: `/Users/jun/Documents/Info 490 Portfolio/arjunsupra_portfolio/public/images/`
3. Copy your photo into this folder
4. Rename it to `profile.jpg`
5. Refresh your browser - the image will appear automatically!

The image will display on the left side of the "about" section on the home page.
